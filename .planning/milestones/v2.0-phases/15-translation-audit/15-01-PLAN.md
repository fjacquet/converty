# Plan 15-01: Missing Keys & Health Calculator Externalization

**Phase:** 15 - Translation Audit
**Sequence:** 1 of 2
**Requirements:** I18N-01, I18N-02 (partial)

## Objective

Add missing translation keys and externalize hardcoded strings from health calculators - the largest category with ~100 strings across 7 files.

## Prerequisites

- Phase 14 (Global Search) complete
- Audit inventories exist (`i18n_audit_inventory.md`, `i18n_missing_keys_inventory.md`)

## Tasks

Add 9 missing keys to all 4 locale files under `calculator.results`:

**File:** `src/lib/converters/health/gfr-calculator.ts`

Change stage strings to keys:

- Return `stage: "stage1"` instead of `"Stage 1: Normal or high GFR"`
- Add stage description keys: `stageDescription`, `stageSeverity`, `stageRecommendation`

**Component:** `src/app/[locale]/health/gfr-calculator/gfr-calculator-component.tsx`

- Translate stage keys at render time

**Translation keys to add:**
