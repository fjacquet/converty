- Servers: result.oldFleet.serverCount | result.newFleet.serverCount | (new - old, show + or -)
- Sockets: result.oldFleet.totalSockets | result.newFleet.totalSockets | delta
- Total Cores: result.oldFleet.totalCores | result.newFleet.totalCores | delta
- Total TDP (W): result.oldFleet.totalTdpW | result.newFleet.totalTdpW | delta
- Total SPECint: result.oldFleet.totalSpecint | result.newFleet.totalSpecint | delta

Format delta cells: prefix "+" for positive, "-" for negative. Use green text for positive (more performance/cores), red for negative TDP increase (more power).
